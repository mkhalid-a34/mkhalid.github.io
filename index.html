Research Initiatives

Public Debt and Infrastructural Development in Sub-Saharan Africa: Heterogeneous Threshold Effect Across Institutional Quality Dimensions

Ongoing Postgraduate Research Focus

This study investigates the non-linear relationship between public borrowing and long-term infrastructure delivery across diverse economies in Sub-Saharan Africa. Utilizing a threshold regression model framework, the research examines how varying structural indices of governance and institutional capacity shift the operational tipping points where public debt transfers from an infrastructural catalyst into a structural constraint. The project intends to offer clear empirical insights that can guide macro-level fiscal policies toward sustainable, growth-driven investment.
